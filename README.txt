Dataset: Solar Power Generation Data
Source: Kaggle - https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/anikannal/solar-power-generation-data

Contents:
Plant_1_Generation_Data.csv        : 15-min inverter-level generation readings, Plant 1
Plant_1_Weather_Sensor_Data.csv    : 15-min plant-level weather sensor readings, Plant 1
Plant_2_Generation_Data.csv        : 15-min inverter-level generation readings, Plant 2
Plant_2_Weather_Sensor_Data.csv    : 15-min plant-level weather sensor readings, Plant 2

Period covered: 15 May 2020 - 17 June 2020 (34 days), two solar plants in India.
Used as-is (unmodified) from the original public Kaggle dataset, for academic/research purposes.
